Welcome to gulaula

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Gulola village is 136112. Gulola village is located in Lakhimpur tehsil of Kheri district in Uttar_pradesh, India. It is situated 27km away from sub-district headquarter Lakhimpur (tehsildar office) and 27km away from district headquarter Kheri. As per 2009 stats, Gulaula is the gram panchayat of Gulola village. The total geographical area of village is 254.12 hectares. Gulola has a total population of 1,509 peoples, out of which male population is 806 while female population is 703. Literacy rate of gulola village is 59.71% out of which 69.73% males and 48.22% females are literate. There are about 298 houses in gulola village. Pincode of gulola village locality is 262701.
